About Us

Texas Tires has grown into more than 30 great locations to serve you. Customer service is our primary goal, and all new tire purchases come with our lifetime free rotation and tire repair warranty.

Texas Tires also has the largest selection of in-stock custom wheels and tires at the lowest prices. We have large showrooms featuring a variety of plus-size wheel and tire options for any vehicle, whether it be street, dirt, or mud, Texas Tires has you covered. We also carry the largest selection of custom wheels and ultra high performance tires in stock. You can drive out the same day with exactly the style, finish, brand, and size of wheels and tires you want. If you need financing for tires and wheels, we can help you with that as well. We offer no hassle buying programs including 90 days same as cash and rent to own programs, with approvals up to $5,000. There is no credit check and no down payment. You can get instantly approved in seconds. Apply online now or visit us at our location located at 3140 Denton Hwy, Haltom City, Texas
